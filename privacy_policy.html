Privacy Policy

LinkGraph is developed using Atlassian Forge technology.

It works inside the cloud on Atlassian servers. The app does not process or store user data outside of Atlassian products and services.

The app doesn't store any personal data.

1. It stores user settings (which cannot identify a person):

  • App theme
  • Graph direction (top->bottom or left->right)
  • Issue fields to show
  • Other settings in future development that are not personal

Example: 

{ "direction": "LR", "theme": "default", "issueFieldKeys": [ "customfield_10011" ] }

2. By default the app logs errors and measures requests. We need to address support issues and improve the app for our clients.

Additionally, your Jira administrator can disable, enable (if previously disabled), or download app logs

3. The app caches keys of related issues to work more quickly.
